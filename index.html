Past life of Wanda

Losing Parents

When she was 10, she and her twin brother, Pietro, were orphaned in a bomb blast that killed their parents, forcing them to grow up in an orphanage in a war-torn country.

Losing Pietro

Pietro Maximoff was a superhuman Sokovian citizen who possessed superhuman speed. He was the older twin brother of Wanda Maximoff. was killed by Ultron whilst saving citizens.

Losing Vision

Wanda used her magic to destroy the Infinity Stone and kill Vision so that Thanos wouldn't be able to attain all the stones. But then Thanos reversed time and killed Vision again.

Westview Anomaly

The Westview Anomaly, also known as the Maximoff Anomaly, was a phenomenon that occurred when after losing vision a grieving Wanda Maximoff unleashed a surge of chaos magic that dispersed a massive energy field surrounding the town of Westview, New Jersey. This energy field, dubbed the Hex,

Wanda sacrificed her Family

Inside the Hex, wanda created her own reality, her own family by controlling the whole town and everybody inside. But when she realized her mistake with the interference of SWORD, she decided to disclose Anomaly. The dissolution of the Anomaly meant that Wanda Maximoff's chaos magic constructs — Vision, Tommy, Billy, and their house — could no longer continue to exist. And she had to sacrifice her family in order to free others.

Power and Uniqueness of Wanda Maximoff

  • Psionics: As a result of exposure to the cosmic energies from the Scepter, which contained the Mind Stone, Maximoff gained an array of psionic powers.
  • Psychic Energy Manipulation: Maximoff can project energy blasts, streams, waves and bolts of her own psionic and telekinetic energy, allowing her to strike, push, pull or blow away her targets,
  • Disintegration: Maximoff is capable of using her energy in such an acute telekinetic way that she is able to completely disintegrate objects, as she did with several Ultron Sentries after sensing her brother's death.
  • Force-Field Generation: Maximoff is able to create highly durable barriers of red energy that she can shape and manipulate as needed.
  • Telekinesis: Maximoff has the ability to move, levitate and otherwise manipulate objects.
  • Levitation: Maximoff is able to use this power to move through the air and simulate flight, in order to hover and safely float back to the ground.
  • Mental Manipulation: Maximoff was able to deploy a form of mental manipulation, often as a type of illusory hypnosis, using the same neuro-electric interface that is the basis of her telepathic powers.
  • Chaos Magic: Able to harness Chaos Magic, Maximoff was the realization of the myth of the Scarlet Witch, a being capable of spontaneous creation.
